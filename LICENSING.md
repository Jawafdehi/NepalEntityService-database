## Why HL3

Jawafdehi.org builds civic-accountability infrastructure for Nepal. We chose the Hippocratic License to align our software with our mission: technology should serve human dignity and justice, never undermine it.

The Hippocratic License is an [Ethical Source](https://ethicalsource.dev) license. It requires that the software not be used to violate human rights laws or the principles in the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights and UN Global Compact.
